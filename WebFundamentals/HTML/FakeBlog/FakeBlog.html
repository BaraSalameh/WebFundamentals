Real Madrid

Founded on 6 March 1902 as Madrid Football Club, the club has traditionally worn a white home kit since inception.
The word real is Spanish for "royal" and was bestowed to the club by King Alfonso XIII in 1920 together with the
royal crown in the emblem.
